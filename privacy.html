Privacy Policy

At Mila Management, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our services.

What Information Do We Collect?

We may collect personal data from you when you use our services, including your name, address, email address, phone number, and other relevant information necessary for providing our services to you. Additionally, we may collect information from publicly available sources or third-party service providers to supplement our records.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your personal data to provide our services to you, such as helping you buy, sell or rent a property. We may also use your information to communicate with you about our services, provide updates, and offer promotional materials that may be of interest to you.

Who Do We Share Your Information With?

We may share your personal data with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services to you, such as property valuers, surveyors, and solicitors. We may also share your information with our business partners to provide joint services to you or as part of a merger or acquisition. We will not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data that we hold. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal data or restrict its use. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. We recommend that you review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal data.
